Interoperable Digital Public Service ()
DESCRIPTION: It is an interoperable public service provisioned via a digital service delivery mode by, or on behalf of, a public administration in support to the realization of a Public Administration digital business capability . An European digital public service comprises any digital public service exposed to a cross-border dimension in the Union.
