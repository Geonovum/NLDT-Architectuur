Disaster Recovery Plan ()
Definition: Architecture Decision Record from where you should specialise the ADR SBBs regarding the Disaster Recovery Plan Source: ISO Source reference: https://www.iso.org/standard/75106.html Additional information: A Disaster Recovery Plan (DRP) is a documented and structured approach that outlines the strategies, processes, and procedures to be followed in the event of a disaster or significant disruption that impacts an organization's IT infrastructure, systems, and data. The primary goal of a DRP is to minimize the impact of the disaster, recover critical systems and data, and restore normal operations as quickly and efficiently as possible.
