Coordination Capacity ()
Definition: The Coordination Capacity Value ABB refers to mediating and bringing different administrative units and dispersed actors to work together to achieve joint actions. It is not only about vertical coordination by hierarchy but also about facilitating horizontal coordination across policy areas and organizations at the same administrative level. Source: Administrative coordination capacity; does the wickedness of policy areas matter?
