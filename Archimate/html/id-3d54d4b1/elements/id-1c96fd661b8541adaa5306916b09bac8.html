Digital Public Service Provider ()
Definition: Digital Public Service Provider ABB is a Business Role representing the entity that offer or deliver public services using digital channels and technologies. Digital public service providers aim to provide efficient and accessible services to citizens and businesses by leveraging the power of digital technology.
