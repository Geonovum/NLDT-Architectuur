Delivery Capacity ()
Definition: The Delivery Capacity Value ABB refers to the ability of public administrations to deliver public services, programs, and policies effectively and efficiently to citizens and stakeholders. It encompasses the overall performance, effectiveness, and responsiveness of public administration in meeting the needs and expectations of the public.
